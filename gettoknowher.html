About Me

Somewhere along the way I've turned illustrating squiggles on Microsoft Paint as a toddler into designing for a career. Miami born, raised and educated, I recently took my talents to Boston and worked as an Editorial Design Co-Op at the Boston Globe until December 2015. I'm also a recent graduate of the University of Miami, where I studied journalism and graphic design.

Come June, I'll be working at the Washington Post in their design department.

You can most likely find me drumming up my next idea; eating croquetas, attempting to knit my 1000th scarf, or doing all three at once.
